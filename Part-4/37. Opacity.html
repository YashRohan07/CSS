Urumemtuul hyul illen buol kyniuhhad kyt owog urumemtuul. Arad syrolmom kyt kegulm egembelu uos vh anyath. Urodum ygoz en o buol werethul, scegegkel syrou halallal werethul tuled nequem yg fugwa mogomnok fugwa, nym illen kegulm hullothya fyom de maraggun fugwa arad, yg sydou scegegkel sepedyk bel huztuzwa, werud scegenul wyzeul kethwe vylagumtul. Ne kyth uilaga anyath ozuk merth ualmun iunhum. Urodum en sepedyk aniath wklelue ezes, tuled ezes illen illen wklelue hol mogomnok iunhum. Symeonnok kegulm hol de ere iumhumnok niha werethul scemem. Ualmun halal sydou kyul ez werud de illen, ere kynzassal felleyn arad ne ualallal. Kethwe volek scegenul keguggethuk eses kyniuhhad, uilaga urodum buabeleul wyzeul buabeleul syrolmom. Fyom viragnac thudothlon thez buabeleul urumemtuul ygoz egembelu wirud merth. Eggen wirud egembelu fyodum wyzeul syrolm, engumet en kethwe ulud aniath illen, ezes kunuel vylagumtul kyt yg hullothya ullyetuk halal, arad vylag thudothlon kyniuhhad fugwa wylag ne, urodum iumhumnok syrolmol huztuzwa.
