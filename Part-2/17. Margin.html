Canada

Canada, the second-largest country in the world, boasts breathtaking landscapes, diverse cultures, and a reputation for welcoming inclusiveness. From stunning national parks to thriving multicultural cities, Canada offers a harmonious blend of natural beauty and vibrant communities.

Brazil

Brazil, the largest country in South America, captivates with its lush Amazon rainforest, vibrant carnival culture, and iconic landmarks like the Christ the Redeemer statue. From samba rhythms to passionate football fandom, Brazil is a country known for its rich heritage and zest for life
